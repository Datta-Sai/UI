ARTS AND SCIENCE COURSES



B.SC.-COMPUTER SCIENCE

The Department of Computer Science was established in 2018 and to educate students to be successful, ethical, and effective problem-solvers and life-long learners who will contribute positively to the economic well-being of our region and nation and who are prepared to tackle complex challenges facing the world.



B.C.A

The Department of Computer Applications was established in 2018 and to the most conductive environment for excellence academic and research oriented undergraduate education in computer science and prepare the students for a globalised technological society and orient them towards serving the society.



B.SC.-MATHEMATICS

The Department of Mathematics was established in 2018 and it has made a steady progress and has been regularly organizing various academic activities. Many outstanding Mathematicians from various parts of this region have visited the Department and delivered lectures. The Department takes an integrated and innovative approach in teaching and training the students. Apart from teaching, the faculty members are carrying out research in the frontier areas of Mathematics.



B.COM

The Department of Commerce was established in the year 2018. The Department has clear objective to prolong academic excellence and endow students with competitive skills in facing the challenges of the volatile business world. Our department augments the ability of the students with humanity, and adopting different teaching-learning strategies like case study, case law analysis, group discussions, role-play, guest lectures, workshops, seminars, E-learning, flip classroom, and gamification, etc. In 2019, the department association was inaugurated in the name of the legend “Philip Kotler.” There are various club activities conducted under this association. It facilitates the students to comprehend the real business environment. The department organizes industry visits and internship programs in every semester. Our department comprises of experienced Faculty Members who are incessantly confident to publish their research articles in the UGC approved journals. They are also motivated and aided to participate in FDP, seminars, webinars, workshops, symposiums, etc.



B.B.A

The Department of Management Studies was established in the academic year 2018-19. The Department offers a 3-year full time BBA Program.Our students are engaged in classes through experiential learning methods like simulation,Modeling, Action oriented leadership, Mini Projects,Management Games, Industry Visits,etc.The faculty members are continuously engaged in converting the student talents into the skills required for the future world. The institution is focused on creating the future Managers, Entrepreneurs, Responsible Leaders and Global citizens.
